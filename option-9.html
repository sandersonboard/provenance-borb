This is the move. The agent does the 90% — population queries, sample size methodology, evidence formatting, tickmark annotations — and gets the 90% right. The 10% that is wrong is judgment-shaped: thresholds, materiality, sign-off chains, override dispositions. The FDE pod owns that 10% and the audit trail captures the seam. Foundation models cannot replace that workflow without replacing the FDE pod, and the FDE pod is the moat.

Each finding ships with the exact text to add. Jamie applies all three in eleven minutes.

The scorer is what shifted Optro Practice from "boutique audit advisory" to "production line." Before it, every workpaper was a one-off; the FDE who delivered it carried its defensibility in their head. After it, defensibility is a number measured against a Big-4-senior persona prompt — repeatable, comparable, improvable. A new FDE pod member onboarding can run the scorer on their own work and feel the heat before the partner does.

The FDE doesn't save Pranav time. Provenance saves Pranav time. The FDE saves Helios the cost of getting it wrong.
