Why two hedges share one repo. The underlying data shape is the same across both scenarios — what changes is the consumer. In SoR-Premium, the Controller and Big-4 partner read the workpaper directly and the moat is the live citation chain on every value. In the Wrapper Era, a foundation-model agent reads the workpaper and the moat is the structured citation envelope around every tool response. Build the audit-grade output layer once; ship it under whichever GTM motion the scenario triggers reward.
