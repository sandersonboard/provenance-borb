RC-047 — Manual journal entry approval — revenue recognition

State: FDE review Assigned: Jamie Okonkwo Defensibility: 92/100 T-minus: 4 days to customer review
Control objective REVIEWED

Manual journal entries posted to revenue accounts are reviewed and approved by an individual independent of the preparer prior to posting, and the threshold for required dual approval is monitored and updated quarterly.

Narrative REVIEWED

Helios Robotics applies a $250,000 single-entry threshold to its manual journal entry approval workflow within NetSuite. Manual entries posted to any of the 47 in-scope revenue ledger accounts that meet or exceed the threshold require a second-level approval from the Controller prior to posting. Entries below the threshold require single approval by a Senior Accountant.

The control owner reviews the threshold quarterly against the materiality benchmark established by the engagement team for the current audit period. The current threshold is supported by memo M-2026-Q1-MJE, signed by the Controller on 2026-01-09.

Sample selection REVIEWED

Methodology agreed in walkthrough with Deloitte engagement team 2026-02-04, memo MEMO-SS-RC-047.
